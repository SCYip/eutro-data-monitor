About The Project

Eutrophication is a process driven by the enrichment of water by nutrients, especially compounds of nitrogen and phosphorus, leading to an increased growth of algae.

Our project aims to create an early warning system. By monitoring key indicators like pH, Temperature, and Turbidity, we can predict potential algal blooms before they become disastrous.

Our Mission

  • Provide affordable water quality monitoring.
  • Enable real-time data access for researchers.
  • Reduce the impact of eutrophication on marine life.
