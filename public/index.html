System Capabilities

Complete Water Analysis

Our hardware suite provides comprehensive data points to ensure a holistic view of water health.

pH Monitoring

Real-time acidity detection. Critical for spotting chemical runoff or biological imbalances immediately.

Thermal Analysis

High-precision DS18B20 sensors track temperature variances that facilitate algal bloom growth.

Turbidity Sensing

Optical measurement of suspended particles. The key indicator for water clarity and quality.

How It Works

From sensor to dashboard in milliseconds.

📡

1. Collect

Sensors capture raw analog data from the water source.

⚡

2. Process
